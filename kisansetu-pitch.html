01 · Problem Statement

India feeds the world.
But its farmers go hungry.

India is an agricultural superpower — yet the farmers at its core are systematically exploited, underserved, and disconnected from the economy they sustain.

The Scale of the Crisis

80%

of India's 140 million farming families own less than 2 acres. Small holdings mean outdated methods, low productivity, and forced rural labour — a vicious cycle with no digital escape route.

No Access to Fair Credit

Formal bank loans require extensive paperwork. Farmers turn to loan sharks who charge exploitative rates, creating debt traps passed down generations.

Middlemen Extract Profits

Multiple layers of intermediaries mean the farmer receives as little as 10–20% of the final retail price of their own produce.

30% Post-Harvest Losses

Poor access to cold storage and transport causes up to 30% of produce to rot before it reaches market — destroying income and food security.

Government Schemes Go Unclaimed

Dozens of central and state schemes exist but most farmers never hear about them or can't navigate the application process alone.

Digital Exclusion

Most agritech apps assume literacy and English. The farmers who need help most are left out — no voice support, no local languages.

A Mental Health Crisis

Farmer suicides account for over 10,000 deaths a year. Debt, crop failure, and isolation create an emergency that is largely ignored.

05 · Implementation Plan

5 days to a working prototype.

MVP scope: a web platform showing the core farmer journey — marketplace, scheme navigator, farm diary, and community forum. Deployed live on Day 5 for a real demo.

Day 1 — Foundation

Architecture & Design System

Finalise MVP scope. Set up Next.js + Supabase. Design the farmer-first UI with local language toggle. Map all core user journeys for the pitch demo.

Day 2 — Core Marketplace

Buy & Sell Flows

Build the produce marketplace (sell side) and input marketplace (buy side). Integrate map view for nearby buyers. Basic user profiles with farm details.

Day 3 — Scheme Navigator & Credit

Welfare & Finance Features

Build the AI-powered scheme eligibility checker and the micro-credit application flow with transparent terms display.

Day 4 — Community & Polish

Forum + Farm Diary + UI Refinement

Add the community forum and digital farm diary. Basic voice navigation demo. Polish all screens and build the pitch deck.

Day 5 — Demo & Pitch

Live Deployment + Presentation

Deploy on Vercel. Walk judges through a live farmer journey: buying inputs → listing produce → checking scheme eligibility → joining the community.
